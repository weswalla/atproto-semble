# How to Contribute

We believe in the magic of diverse minds collaborating openly, so whether you're a researcher with ideas about what would make Semble more useful, a developer excited about ATProto, or a tools-for-thought tinkerer — we’d love to connect!

## Ways to Contribute

- **Come chat with us** in our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/SHvvysb73e)
- **Join our [mailing list](https://cosmik.network/#connect)** to stay updated
- **Check out [our roadmap](https://github.com/orgs/cosmik-network/projects/1)** to see where we're headed and what we're prioritizing
- **Report issues** on [Github](https://github.com/cosmik-network/semble/issues) or [Tangled](https://tangled.org/@cosmik.network/semble)

### Things We're Particularly Excited About

- **Integrations** with bookmarking apps, reference managers, or tools for thought like Obsidian
- **Feedback, feature requests and ideas** to help us understand what's working and what's missing

## Development Setup

We're currently in the process of adding public documentation, which will include detailed setup guidelines and contribution workflows. In the meantime, feel free to reach out on Discord/email or directly on Github and Tangled if you're interested in contributing code!

## Questions?

Reach out at [hello@cosmik.network](mailto:hello@cosmik.network) or find us on [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/cosmik.network)
